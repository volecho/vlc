Elena Deva Unleashes Catharsis on “Abusement Park”

The following feature is now included in our online magazine which is also available in print.

Issue #13

Online Magazine | Print Magazine

Los Angeles-based alternative rock artist Elena Deva is back with a new, impressive single titled “Abusement Park,” a dark, emotionally charged song that delves deep into the psychology of manipulation and survival. Recorded in Los Angeles, “Abusement Park” embodies Elena Deva’s signature intensity, transforming personal experiences into a cinematic, emotionally charged journey.

The song “Abusement Park” starts off with tense, brooding guitars, immediately establishing a sense of tension and foreboding. Elena Deva’s deep, commanding voice takes center stage, delivering lyrics that are confessional yet confrontational in nature. Throughout the song, the instrumentation becomes increasingly layered and urgent, culminating in a strong violin-filled climax, letting out all the tension built up in the song. This climax is less a musical flourish and more a breakthrough, a coming out of darkness into the light.



While drawing inspiration from the alternative rock of the early 2000s, Elena Deva adds a modern touch to her music, echoing the raw, emotional energy of alternative rock artists of the time. Her songwriting does not shy away from confronting the uncomfortable; instead, she turns it into a form of empowerment. “Abusement Park” is a song about being emotionally manipulated, yet it is also a declaration of survival, a song for people who have found power in the other side of the coin.

What makes Elena Deva unique in the alternative rock scene is her ability to create immersive, layered sounds, instead of opting for minimalist approaches to songwriting. Her music is expansive, yet deeply personal, making it all the more appealing to listeners seeking authenticity in their music.

With her debut album set to arrive in 2026, Elena Deva is steadily establishing herself as a force to be reckoned with in the alternative rock scene. If “Abusement Park” is any indication of what is to come, Elena Deva’s debut promises to be a raw, bold, and emotionally charged journey, a journey of self-discovery and empowerment.
