OGGY Finds Light in Motion on Help Me Find a Reason

London-based OGGY enters 2026 with the release of “Help Me Find a Reason,” a pop-rock indie track that feels like it was made for the open road, the late night drive home, and the moment when uncertainty becomes determination. The song was written by OGGY alone, starting as a simple acoustic piece and evolving into something more complete and enlightened. The end result is a song that has a sense of forward motion, with the guitars driving forward while the melody stays firmly planted.

The strength of the song lies in its sense of purpose. OGGY writes from the heart, but the heart is open. The song deals with the importance of faith, not necessarily in anything or anyone, but in the idea of forward motion. OGGY’s vocals are strong and warm, the result of years of training and live performances throughout the city of London.

Fans of Florence Welch from her earlier days, Avril Lavigne from her more introspective periods, or the latest pop-rock indie releases will find a lot to love here. There is also a sense of cinematic wonder, as if the closing scene from a coming-of-age film where the protagonist steps out into the world.

“Help Me Find a Reason” is a great introduction to OGGY’s upcoming EP, a sense of tone that is based firmly in optimism and determination. It is a sense of an artist who is comfortable with her own growth and ready to take the world by storm.
