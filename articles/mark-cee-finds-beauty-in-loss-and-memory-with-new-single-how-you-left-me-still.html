Mark Cee Finds Beauty in Loss and Memory With New Single "How You Left Me Still"

Many songs are produced for entertaining purposes. However, other songs are born out of necessity. The latest single from Mark Cee titled "How You Left Me Still" belongs to the latter category. Released on June 15, 2026, the song is dedicated to grief, memory, and those individuals whose absence is always felt long after their departure. The song was inspired by losses experienced by the singer. Specifically, it reflects on the death of his father-in-law and his father, whose picture accompanies the singer on the single artwork. One of the hardest things in life is the sudden disappearance of an individual who helped shape us as a person. Instead of making grandiose statements about sadness, Mark Cee touches upon that fragile moment when one realizes how much the absence of this person means. How You Left Me Still reflects all those qualities of the ambient indie folk artist that have been steadily developing throughout his career. Thus, the composition features the careful buildup of emotions and natural expression of the feelings of grief and pain. The instruments of choice include gentle guitars that provide a solid background for the song along with carefully chosen strings and flute pieces. Another great quality of How You Left Me Still is the singer's devotion to the "human" way of recording. In the era of digital processing that can perfect the sound of any composition, Mark Cee keeps his dedication to the old ways. In this way, the recording sounds warm, honest and authentic, as if the listener hears something personal rather than a professionally done song. For fans of the singer, as well as for fans of artists such as Nick Drake, Sufjan Stevens, Iron & Wine, and Damien Rice, How You Left Me Still is going to be an exciting composition. Those who appreciate such albums as Carrie & Lowell or Pink Moon should definitely pay attention to the single too since it can be compared to them. In its turn, the themes touched upon in the song remind listeners of such movies as Manchester by the Sea, A Ghost Story, and The Father. As those movies demonstrate, the grief does not usually sound loud and noisy. It comes quietly, revealing itself in the form of absence, memories, and various reminders. However, the single is not only a composition that reveals something personal about the singer. It also serves as an introduction to the new album released by Mark Cee called It's Me, Mark Cee. This title of the album reflects the fact that the singer focuses on personal identity and stories that shaped his life, his music and emotions. Drawing on his experience in writing songs and poetry, starting from 1995, Mark Cee touches upon his experience of being a part of someone's life. The music created by him contains the weight of true moments, relationships, and memories of those times. The reason why How You Left Me Still is especially touching is its lack of resolutions. While the song does not try to provide some kind of solutions to grief and loss or teach a lesson, it shows the truth that some people leave behind gaps that cannot be filled. Learning how to live with that absence becomes a part of the story. In the times when everyone tends to move on, Mark Cee provides listeners with a different opportunity. He lets grief exist without any apologies. He leaves the place for memories to come. In this way, he composes a song that will become relevant for all those people who want to have one more chat with someone, say one more goodbye or just spend one more moment together. Thus, How You Left Me Still is an impressive piece of songwriting. Being thoughtful and emotionally rich, it touches upon important themes of grief and memory.
